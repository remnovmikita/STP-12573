About a Game

It's match-3, but with elements that actually do different things.
So we basically took your standard match-3 game but made the elemental stuff actually mean something instead of just being pretty window dressing. Each tile represents a natural force, and combining them creates visually engaging reactions.
Don't let the easy start fool you - by level 20, you'll be planning moves three steps ahead.
